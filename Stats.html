Common Mental Health Disorders

According to National Alliance on Mental Illness data, millions of Americans experience mental illness each year. It's important to understand that no one is alone. Statistics on mental health help increase public awareness, reduce stigma, and drive better care options

Mental Health By The Numbers

  • Annual prevelance among US adults by condition:
    1. Anxiety Disorders (estimated 48 million people)
    2. Major Depressive Episode (estimated 21 million people)
    3. Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (estimated 9 million people)
    4. Bipolar Disorder (estimated 7 million people)
    5. Borderline Personality Disorder (estimated 3.5 million people)
    6. Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (estimated 3 million people)
    7. Schizophrenia (estimated 1.5 million people)
